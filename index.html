How the App Works

📳

Shake to Reveal

Give your iPhone a real shake. CoreMotion detects the gesture and the ball bounces to life with your answer.

🎙️

Speak Your Question

Hold the microphone button and ask aloud. Your question appears live on screen as you speak using on-device speech recognition.

🔮

Color-Coded Answers

The oracle's inner eye glows green for positive answers, amber for neutral, and red when the outlook is grim.

☕️

Tip the Oracle

Love the app? Support it from inside using the built-in tip jar — Kind Soul, True Believer, or Cosmic Patron.

Frequently Asked Questions

Make sure your iPhone isn't lying flat on a table. Hold it naturally in your hand and give it a firm, short shake — like a cocktail shaker. If it still doesn't respond, try restarting the app. The shake threshold requires a quick jolt of about 2.5g.
Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone and make sure Magic 8 Ball is enabled. Also check Settings → Privacy & Security → Speech Recognition. Both permissions are required for the voice feature to work.
If the payment sheet closed without a confirmation, you were not charged. All purchases are processed directly by Apple and are subject to their refund policy. For any billing concerns, visit reportaproblem.apple.com.
Refunds for in-app purchases are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find the purchase, and tap "Report a Problem" to submit a refund request.
The core 8 Ball experience — shaking and getting answers — works fully offline. Speech recognition may use Apple's servers for best results, though on-device fallback is available. The tip jar requires an internet connection to process payments.
Magic 8 Ball requires iOS 16 or later and is compatible with iPhone 8 and newer. The shake feature uses the built-in accelerometer, which is present on all supported devices.
