How the App Works

📳

Shake to Reveal

Give your iPhone a real shake. CoreMotion detects the gesture and the ball bounces to life with your answer.

🎙️

Speak Your Question

Hold the microphone button and ask aloud. Your question appears live on screen as you speak using on-device speech recognition.

🔮

Color-Coded Answers

The oracle's inner eye glows green for positive answers, amber for neutral, and red when the outlook is grim.

☕️

Tip the Oracle

Love the app? Support it from inside using the built-in tip jar — Kind Soul, True Believer, or Cosmic Patron.

Frequently Asked Questions

Make sure your iPhone isn't lying flat on a table. Hold it naturally in your hand and give it a firm, short shake — like a cocktail shaker. If it still doesn't respond, try restarting the app. The shake threshold requires a quick jolt of about 2.5g.
Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone and make sure Magic 8 Ball is enabled. Also check Settings → Privacy & Security → Speech Recognition. Both permissions are required for the voice feature to work.
If the payment sheet closed without a confirmation, you were not charged. All purchases are processed directly by Apple and are subject to their refund policy. For any billing concerns, visit reportaproblem.apple.com.
Refunds for in-app purchases are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find the purchase, and tap "Report a Problem" to submit a refund request.
The core 8 Ball experience — shaking and getting answers — works fully offline. Speech recognition may use Apple's servers for best results, though on-device fallback is available. The tip jar requires an internet connection to process payments.
Magic 8 Ball requires iOS 16 or later and is compatible with iPhone 8 and newer. The shake feature uses the built-in accelerometer, which is present on all supported devices.

🔮 Privacy Policy

Magic 8 Ball  ·  Effective Date: June 18, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 18, 2026

🛡️ The App collects no personal data. Support form submissions are used solely to respond to your inquiry.

1 · Overview

Magic 8 Ball ("the App", "we", "our") is a mobile entertainment application for iPhone developed and operated as an independent app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights as a user. We are committed to full compliance with Apple's App Store Review Guidelines, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and all applicable privacy laws.

2 · Information We Collect

Through the App itself, Magic 8 Ball is designed with privacy as a default. The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share:

  • Your location or GPS data
  • Your device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, or similar)
  • Your contacts, photos, or files
  • Your usage behavior, analytics, or crash logs via any third-party tracker
  • Any data for advertising or marketing purposes

Through this support website, we collect the following information only when you voluntarily submit the contact form:

  • Your name — so we know how to address you in our reply
  • Your email address — so we can respond to your message
  • Your message and subject — the content of your support request

This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not used for marketing, shared with advertisers, or added to any mailing list. You may request deletion of this data at any time by contacting us.

3 · Microphone & Speech Recognition

The App includes an optional voice input feature that uses Apple's Speech Recognition framework (SFSpeechRecognizer) and AVFoundation to transcribe your spoken questions in real time.

  • Microphone access is only active while you are actively holding the microphone button. It stops the moment you release it.
  • Audio is never recorded or stored by the App. No audio files are saved to your device or transmitted to our servers.
  • Speech processing may be performed by Apple's servers or on-device, depending on your iOS settings and device capabilities. This processing is governed entirely by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • You will be prompted to grant microphone and speech recognition permissions before the feature activates. You may revoke these permissions at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security.

4 · Motion & Accelerometer Data

The App uses your device's accelerometer via Apple's CoreMotion framework to detect shake gestures. This motion data is processed entirely on-device in real time and is never stored, logged, or transmitted. No CoreMotion data leaves your device.

5 · In-App Purchases

The App offers optional consumable in-app purchases ("tips") processed exclusively through Apple's StoreKit framework. All payment transactions are handled entirely by Apple. We never see, receive, or store your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID. Purchase records are managed by Apple and subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

For refund requests, visit reportaproblem.apple.com.

6 · Data Shared With Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes. The only third-party interactions are:

  • Apple Inc. — for payment processing (StoreKit), speech recognition (SFSpeechRecognizer), and operating system services. These are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • EmailJS — when you submit the contact form on this support page, your name, email address, and message are transmitted via EmailJS to deliver your message to us. EmailJS acts as a delivery service only and does not use your data for any other purpose. See EmailJS Privacy Policy.

Your email address is used exclusively to reply to your support request. It is never added to a mailing list, shared with advertisers, or used for any purpose beyond responding to you.

7 · Children's Privacy

Magic 8 Ball is a general-audience entertainment app. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Economic Area). The App does not contain advertising, behavioral tracking, or social features. If you believe a child has inadvertently submitted personal information via our support form, please contact us immediately at the address below so we may delete it.

8 · Data Security

Since the App does not collect or store personal data, there is no user data at risk from our side. All sensitive operations (payments, speech processing) are handled by Apple's secure infrastructure. This support website is delivered over HTTPS.

9 · Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access — request a copy of any data we hold about you
  • Right to Erasure — request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Object — object to processing of your data
  • Right to Portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Do Not Sell My Data — we do not sell your data. Ever.

Since Magic 8 Ball does not collect personal data through the App itself, these rights primarily apply to any information you voluntarily submit via this support website's contact form. To exercise any right, contact us below.

10 · Cookies & Tracking

The Magic 8 Ball app contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics trackers, no cookies, and no fingerprinting scripts. This support website also does not use cookies or tracking technologies of any kind.

11 · Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App or applicable law. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

12 · Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please reach out using the contact form on this page or by scrolling up to the Get in Touch section.

We will respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 30 days.
